Company Profile

Ijese Tech Lab is the technology lab of Ijese Innovative Services and Supplies Ltd registered with CAC Reg No. RC-1417945.

We have highly creative and experienced team of Computer Science graduates with over 10 years experience in software engineering, web and mobile application development, FinTech (Financial Technology) application, Artificial Intelligence (AI) systems, Machine Learning (ML), Virtual and Augmented Reality (VR/AR), Internet of Things (IoT) and Robotics. We also have great Project Management and Product Management leads in our team.

We deliver full-cycle mobile and web application design and development services of any complexity as well as VR, AR, MR, AI, IoT, and UI/UX services.

We look forward to bringing your project to life efficiently and cost effectively.

Please feel free to reach out or stop by for a chat whenever you have questions or need any support.
